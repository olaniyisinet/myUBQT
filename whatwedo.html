What We Do

CUSTOMER ENAGEMENT (UBQT Engage)

UBQT Engage is a scalable and flexible solution with built-in counterfeit protection that;

  • Combines our marketing expertise and emerging technologies to help brands engage with their customers, build loyalty and grow market share.
  • Creates a database of loyal customer for brands, enable brands engage customers in a highly personalised way and incentivise customers to buy.
  • Provides reporting that gives brands visibility of their campaigns performance as well as customer insights.


How it works

Brand @ Production/Manufacturing
  • Label application (human or machine readable depending on requirements)
Consumer @ Point of purchase
  • Scratch/Peel
  • Text/Scan (Who, What, Where and When sent to Brand)
  • Verify (Confirmation received by consumer, protection from counterfeit products)
  • Earn Rewards
Brand @ Marketing/Sales/Management
  • Reporting/Analytics (helps brands make smarter, data driven decisions)

PRODUCT VERIFICATION (UBQT Verify)

UBQT Verify is a web and mobile solution that enables brands protect brand equity of their products and manage consumer trust by helping consumers avoid dangerous counterfeit products in the marketplace. Our solution can help;

  • Pinpoint location of suspected counterfeit products.
  • Map key metrics like time to market, number of verifications in a location etc. and help brands identify inefficiencies.
  • Discourage counterfeiters and Increase product sales.


How it works

Brand @ Production/Manufacturing
  • Label application (human or machine readable depending on requirements)
Consumer @ Point of purchase
  • Scratch/Peel
  • Text/Scan (Who, What, Where and When sent to Brand)
  • Verify (Confirmation received by consumer, protection from counterfeit products)
Brand @ Marketing/Sales/Management
  • Reporting/Analytics (helps brands make smarter, data driven decisions)

TRACK & TRACE SOLUTION (UBQT Track)

UBQT Track is a comprehensive track and trace solution that helps brands secure and monitor their supply chains from when the product is manufactured till it is in the hands of the consumer. Our solution can help;

  • Create chronological chain of events and enable complete chain visibility and historical reporting.
  • Ensure transparency of product movements, trace product diversion and identify vulnerable points in the supply chain.
  • Gain insights onto optimizing protection of brand, security of products and cost efficiency in distribution chaingmail.com .


How it works

Brand @ Production/Manufacturing
  • Label application (machine readable)
Brand @ Supply chain
  • Distributor/Wholesalers (Who, What, Where and When sent to Brand)
  • Retailer (Who, What, Where and When sent to Brand)
Brand @ Logistics/Management
  • Reporting/Analytics (helps brands make smarter, data driven decisions)
